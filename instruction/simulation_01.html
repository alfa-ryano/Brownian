Instruction #01

You will have to click the "START" button in the bottom left-corner to start this problem. There are two charts in the right panel: asset price chart (upper right panel) and portfolio value chart (lower right panel). The blue line in the portfolio value chart shows your updated portfolio value and the green line shows the updated benchmark portfolio value.

Parameters #01

Initial fund = [portfolio] ECU; Maturity period = [period/60] minute(s); Money market interest = [interest]%; Initial asset price = [price]; Fixed compensation = [fixed_comp]_comp]; Additional compensation = [add_comp]%; Investor's allocation strategy in the asset market = [b_asset]%; Investor's allocation strategy in the money market = [1 - b_asset]%
